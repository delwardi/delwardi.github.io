Here are some tips to help you stay safer in the future:

Tip #1: Stop, Look, Think

Did anything appear unusual? Did you recognize the sender's address? Was it similar to an official email but not exactly the same?

If in doubt, exercise caution and scrutinize the email further.

Tip #2: Do you spot a red flag?

At Digimind, any email from an external sender should have a caution message at the very top. Keep an eye out for this banner.

If the email lacks the expected banner, consider it suspicious.

Tip #3: When in doubt, throw it out

If you ever suspect that an email is suspicious, it's better to be cautious. Forward it to security@digimind.com for further investigation.
